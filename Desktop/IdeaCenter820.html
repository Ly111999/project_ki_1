Feature

Tiny Footprint, Big Statement


At 96% smaller than a traditional desktop tower, the IdeaCenter 820 is extremely compact. This tiny desktop fits just about anywhere and can be positioned horizontally or vertically—mount it on a wall, behind a monitor, or even under a desk. You can even power digital signage with a PC that’s easy to hide from view. It delivers reliable, professional-level performance, while freeing up valuable desk-space.

The Benefits of Modular Design


With the IdeaCenter 820, you can create your own modular all-in-one PC. Simply use the Tiny inside the back of the IdeaCenter Tiny-in-One (TIO) display. Keep costs down when choosing to upgrade the display and computing power separately, and get more use from your assets over time.

Packs a Punch


The IdeaCenter 820 features a small footprint yet delivers heavyweight performance. With up to six available independent displays, this PC expands Ideal for financial and creative industries—and all your intensive multimedia, multitasking needs.

Bigger Isn't Necessarily Better


Don’t be deceived by its miniature frame, as the IdeaCenter 820 punches well above its weight. Equipped with a 7th generation Intel® Core™ and PCIe SSD storage, you can power up in a flash and load files even faster. Intensive tasks like multimedia creation, graphic design, and number-crunching are a walk in the park.

Help Is Just a Keystroke Away


With high-performance Intel® Core™ vPro™ processors, your IT team can remotely access your fleet of desktops. They can easily roll out software upgrades or fixes to all PCs on the network, saving on both management costs and desk-side visit time.

Lifecyle Stability


With Intel® Stable Image Platform Program (SIPP), your IdeaCenter 820 is guaranteed to have platform availability for at least 12 months. So you can manage your PC lifecycle and keep costs down.
